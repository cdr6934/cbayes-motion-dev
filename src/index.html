In the beginning there was....

The world was without form, and void; and darkness was upon the face of the deep. And the Spirit of God moved upon the face of the waters.

And God said,

Let there be light and there was light

Then there was light

And God saw the light, that it was good: and God divided the light from the darkness.

And God said,

Let the waters under the heaven be gathered together unto one place, and let the dry land appear: and it was so.
